About Petal and Leaf Cake Studio

Welcome to Petal and Leaf Cake Studio. I make custom cakes and cupcakes out of my home in Centerville, Ohio.

I enjoy combining the visual beauty of flowers and the wonder of nature with the deliciousness of cake and frosting!    Mmmmmmm!
Once complete, I love sharing them with others!!

I can’t wait to create something special for you!

Flavors & Ingredients

My signature flavor is Carrot Cake with  vanilla buttercream frosting and flowers.

I also have a really good, lemon cake with extra lemony frosting and buttercream flowers.

Of course I can do chocolate, yellow, white, red velvet, cherry, strawberry, spice, coconut… I start with a boxed cake mix and zhuzh it up a bit, so whatever’s out there is an option!

American Buttercream: Butter, confectioner’s sugar, flavor (vanilla extract, lemon juice, etc.)

Swiss Buttercream: Butter, Egg whites (pasteurized to at least 160⁰ F), sugar, flavor (vanilla extract, lemon juice, cream cheese, etc.)
