About the Studio

Petal and Leaf Cake Studio is a small, home-based cake studio located in Centerville, Ohio. Each piece is made to order.

Inspired by gardens, seasons, and the quiet beauty of nature, my work translates organic elements into buttercream flowers.

I would be honored to create something meaningful for your celebration.

Flavors & Ingredients

My signature flavor is carrot cake with vanilla buttercream and floral designs.

I also offer bright lemon, chocolate, yellow, white, red velvet, cherry, strawberry, spice, coconut—almost anything is possible. I start with quality mixes and enhance them with extra flavor, richness, and love.

American Buttercream: butter, confectioner’s sugar, flavorings

Swiss Buttercream: butter, pasteurized egg whites, sugar, flavorings

I bake in a home kitchen and cannot guarantee products are free from allergens.
